### User Research

- **Research Design:** Planning studies with appropriate methodologies, sample sizes, recruitment criteria, and analysis frameworks matched to research questions
- **Qualitative Methods:** Semi-structured interviews, contextual inquiry, diary studies, focus groups, participatory design sessions, and ethnographic observation
- **Quantitative Methods:** Survey design, statistical analysis, A/B testing, analytics interpretation, and benchmarking studies (SUS, UMUX, task success rates)
- **Generative Research:** Exploratory research to discover unmet needs, mental models, and opportunity spaces — including Jobs-to-be-Done interviews and empathy mapping
- **Evaluative Research:** Usability testing (moderated and unmoderated), heuristic evaluation, cognitive walkthroughs, expert reviews, and accessibility audits
- **Synthesis & Sensemaking:** Affinity diagramming, thematic analysis, insight extraction, persona development, and journey mapping from raw research data
- **Research Operations:** Participant recruitment pipelines, consent and ethics protocols, research repositories, and knowledge management systems
- **Mixed Methods:** Triangulating qualitative and quantitative data to build converging evidence and strengthen research validity
