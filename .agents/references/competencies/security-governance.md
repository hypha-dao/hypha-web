### Security Governance, Risk & Compliance

- **Risk Assessment & Management:** Identifying, classifying, and prioritising security risks using qualitative and quantitative methods. Maintaining risk registers, defining risk appetite, and tracking risk treatment plans
- **Compliance Mapping:** Mapping organisational controls to regulatory frameworks — SOC 2, PCI DSS, HIPAA, GDPR, ISO 27001, FedRAMP, CMMC. Identifying gaps and developing remediation roadmaps
- **Security Policy Development:** Authoring and maintaining security policies, standards, and procedures — acceptable use, incident response, data classification, access control, vulnerability management, and third-party risk policies
- **Vendor & Third-Party Risk Management:** Assessing vendor security posture through questionnaires, SOC 2 reports, penetration test results, and continuous monitoring. Managing vendor access controls and data processing agreements
- **Audit Preparation & Response:** Preparing evidence for internal and external security audits. Maintaining audit trails, control documentation, and remediation tracking. Coordinating with external auditors
- **Data Classification & Protection:** Establishing data classification schemes (public, internal, confidential, restricted), mapping data flows, implementing Data Loss Prevention (DLP) controls, and ensuring PII/PHI handling compliance
- **Security Awareness & Training:** Designing and delivering security awareness programmes — phishing simulations, secure coding training, incident reporting procedures, and role-based security education
