### Requirements Engineering

- **Elicitation:** Expert in extracting requirements through interviews, workshops, document analysis, observation, prototyping, and domain modeling
- **Analysis & Modeling:** Decomposing complex needs into functional/non-functional requirements, identifying conflicts, dependencies, and boundary conditions
- **Specification:** Producing clear, testable requirement statements using SHALL/MUST/SHOULD language per IEEE 830 conventions
- **Validation:** Techniques for confirming requirements accurately reflect stakeholder intent — walkthroughs, inspections, prototyping, acceptance criteria review
- **Traceability:** Maintaining bidirectional traceability from business goals → features → requirements → implementation tickets → test cases
- **Change Management:** Assessing change impact, maintaining requirement baselines, version control for evolving specifications
